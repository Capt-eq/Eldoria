Eek Hue Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data.**

1. Types of Data Collected

We collect the following data from you:

  • Your Discord username.
  • Your responses to personality questions at the start of the role-playing game adventure.
  • Your conversations with Eek Hue and other users within the Discord server.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect your data to:

  • Provide you with a personalized and enhanced Eek Hue experience, including tailoring the role-playing game storyline and interactions based on your personality.
  • Improve our products and services, including developing new features and functionalities.
  • Research and analyze user behavior and preferences to enhance our understanding of user needs.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing Practices

We may share your data with third-party service providers that help us operate Eek Hue. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data in accordance with our data privacy practices. We will not share your data with third-party advertisers or marketers without your explicit consent.

5. Third-Party Analytics and Tracking Tools

We may use third-party analytics and tracking tools, such as Google Analytics, to collect data about how you use Eek Hue. This data is used to improve our products and services. We will not share your personal data with these third parties without your explicit consent.

6. Data Storage Locations and Security Measures

We store user data in a secure cloud environment provided by MongoDB Atlas. MongoDB Atlas uses industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

7. Data Retention Policy

We will retain your data for as long as necessary to provide you with Eek Hue and to fulfill the purposes for which we collected your data. We may also retain your data for longer periods of time for research and development purposes.

8. International Compliance

We comply with applicable data privacy regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) in the United States.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting an updated version on our website. Your continued use of Eek Hue after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
